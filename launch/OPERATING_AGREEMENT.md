## ARTICLE 1: FORMATION

1.1 **Name:** PalmFi Lending LLC (the "Company")
1.2 **Jurisdiction:** State of Delaware
1.3 **Principal Office:** [Your address]
1.4 **Purpose:** Consumer lending, loan origination, financial technology
1.5 **Term:** Perpetual unless dissolved per Article 8

## ARTICLE 2: MEMBERS

2.1 **Initial Member:** [Your name], holding 100% membership interest
2.2 **Capital Contribution:** $[amount] initial capital contribution
2.3 **Additional Contributions:** Members may contribute additional capital
    upon unanimous consent
2.4 **No Right to Withdraw:** No member has the right to withdraw capital
    without consent of all members

## ARTICLE 3: MANAGEMENT

3.1 **Manager-Managed:** The Company shall be managed by a Manager
3.2 **Initial Manager:** [Your name], [Your title]
3.3 **Manager Powers:** The Manager has full authority to:
    a) Bind the Company in contracts
    b) Open and manage bank accounts
    c) Hire employees and contractors
    d) Originate loans and set lending terms
    e) Enter into agreements with service providers
3.4 **Limitations:** Manager may not without member consent:
    a) Sell substantially all assets
    b) Merge or dissolve the Company
    c) Incur debt exceeding $[limit] without approval

## ARTICLE 4: ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS

4.1 **Profits and Losses:** Allocated proportionally to membership interest
4.2 **Distributions:** Made at Manager's discretion, subject to:
    a) Payment of all Company obligations
    b) Reserve for loan loss provisions
    c) Regulatory capital requirements
4.3 **Tax Treatment:** Company elected to be taxed as [disregarded entity / S-Corp]

## ARTICLE 5: BOOKS AND RECORDS

5.1 The Company shall maintain:
    a) Complete financial records
    b) Loan portfolio performance data
    c) Regulatory filings and correspondence
    d) Minutes of material decisions
5.2 Members have access to records upon reasonable notice

## ARTICLE 6: INDEMNIFICATION

6.1 The Company shall indemnify Manager and members for acts
    taken in good faith on behalf of the Company
6.2 No indemnification for fraud, willful misconduct, or
    criminal activity

## ARTICLE 7: DISSOLUTION

7.1 The Company dissolves upon:
    a) Unanimous consent of members
    b) Judicial dissolution
    c) Event causing dissolution under Delaware law
7.2 Upon dissolution, assets distributed:
    a) First: Pay creditors (including loan obligations)
    b) Second: Establish reserve for pending claims
    c) Third: Distribute to members per their interests

## ARTICLE 8: MISCELLANEOUS

8.1 **Governing Law:** Delaware
8.2 **Amendments:** Requires written consent of all members
8.3 **Severability:** Invalid provisions severed; remainder enforceable
8.4 **Entire Agreement:** Supersedes all prior agreements

# PalmFi — Lender Partnership Outreach Targets

*Alternative to getting your own license: operate as a service provider under an existing Virginia-regulated or nationally-chartered lender.*

## How to Structure a Partnership

**PalmFi provides:**
- Technology platform (application, scoring, payment portal, admin dashboard)
- AI underwriting engine (AUC 0.756)
- Servicing & collections automation
- Borrower acquisition (marketing/sourcing)

**Licensed partner provides:**
- Their existing lending license (Virginia or national bank charter)
- Legal compliance oversight
- Loan funding (or co-funding with PalmFi)

**Revenue split:** Partner takes 10-30% of loan revenue as "license fee"

## Tips for Outreach

1. **Target Director/VP level** — Business Development or Strategic Partnerships titles
2. **Mention your AUC 0.71+** — this is your differentiator
3. **Offer a live demo** — let them see the borrower flow
4. **Be upfront about volume** — small pilot first, prove it, then scale
5. **Have your compliance packet ready** — business plan, contract template, privacy policy
6. **Don't lead with "we need your license"** — lead with "we can make you more money with zero tech investment"
7. **Ask for a 15-minute call** — low ask, easy to accept

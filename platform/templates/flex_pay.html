Flexible Payment Options

Choose the payment plan that fits your life. No penalties, no surprises — just options that work for you.

Standard Monthly

Fixed payment every month. Simple and predictable — you always know what you owe.

  • Fixed amount, same day each month
  • Full amortization schedule provided upfront
  • No prepayment penalties
  • Terms from 6 to 60 months

Bi-Weekly

Pay every two weeks. You'll make 26 half-payments per year — that's one extra payment annually, reducing your balance faster.

  • 26 payments per year (vs 12 monthly)
  • Pay off your loan faster with no extra cost
  • Aligns with common pay schedules
  • Switch anytime at no charge

Income Share

Pay a percentage of your actual income. Earn less, pay less. Earn more, pay it off faster. Flexible when you need it most.

  • Pay a fixed % of your monthly income
  • Lower payments in low-income months
  • Capped at 150% of standard payment
  • Switch between standard and income-share

How Income-Share Works

Low income month

You pay less — a fixed % of your actual earnings. Never more than 150% of your standard payment.

High income month

You pay more (up to 150% of standard). The extra reduces your balance faster.

Risk tier matters

Better credit = lower share %. Tier A borrowers pay just 5% of income. Tier E pays 15%.
